About NH

The History of HN

The H. N, Abuja opened its doors in 2015, becoming the first Nuca property in Nigeria. The hotel is located in the heart of Abuja's central business district, just minutes from government offices, embassies, and cultural attractions. The hotel was designed by award-winning architects and interior designers, with a focus on blending modern luxury with traditional Nigerian style. The result is a stunning property that reflects the rich culture and history of the region..

Great Location

The hotel is easily accessible by car or taxi, and is located just 40 minutes from Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport. Whether you're here for business or pleasure, The H. N, Abuja is the perfect home base for exploring all that Abuja has to offer.

Free Meals

The H. N, Abuja have multiple dining options available on-site, ranging from casual to fine dining. These restaurants offer a variety of cuisines, including Nigerian, Mediterranean, and Asian, among others. The hotel also offers 24-hour room service, so you can enjoy a meal in the comfort of your own room

Fitness Room

The H. N, Abuja has a state-of-the-art fitness center that is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. The fitness center features a variety of cardio and strength training equipment, including treadmills, ellipticals, and free weights. The center also has a dedicated stretching area, as well as a separate room for yoga and Pilates.
